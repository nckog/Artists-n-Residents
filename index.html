Artists
'n'
Residents

Care homes are very quiet these days. Residents have no outings, very few visitors, very little to do and no live music.


Meanwhile, with concert halls closed, the orchestras are silent. Musicians up and down the country can't work, can't perform.


Artists 'n' Residents brings the joy of live music, directly from the homes of some of the UK's most loved and respected musicians, straight to care home residents, every week.
